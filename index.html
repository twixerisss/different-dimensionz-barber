About Us

A Different Dimension
of Barbering

Different Dimensionz Barber Shop brings something unique to Wellingborough High Street. Our NVQ Level 2 qualified barbers combine technical skill with creative flair to deliver cuts that stand out.

From classic gentleman's cuts to the latest trends and creative designs, we bring a different dimension to barbering. Every client gets a personalised consultation to ensure we deliver exactly the look you're after.

NVQ Level 2 qualified barbers delivering creative, precision cuts with a personal touch.

We believe great barbering is about more than just a haircut - it's about the experience, the attention to detail, and walking out feeling like the best version of yourself.
